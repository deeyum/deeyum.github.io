Tumko bhi hai khabar
Mujhko bhi hai pata
Ho raha hai juda
Donon ka raasta
Door jaakar bhi
tum meri
Yaadon mein rehna
Kabhi alvida na kehna....


You know, as well as I,
That our paths are moving away
Even when you go far away,
Remain in my memories
Never say goodbye....
